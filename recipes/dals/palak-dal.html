Palak Dal

🕒 30 MINS ⭐ BEGINNER 🌿 FRESH

Prep & Ingredients

COOKER
  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Spinach 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Nos
TADKA
  • Oil/Ghee 2 tbsp
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Onion 1 Small
✅ PREP
  • Wash Dal 3x
  • Wash Palak 2x
  • Chop Veg Done
00:00

TAP A TASK TO START

The Deep Clean5 MINS

Wash Dal 3 times. Soak spinach to remove sand. Slit green chilies and chop tomatoes.

Pressure Cook15 MINS

Add all cooker items + 3 cups water. No salt! 3-4 whistles on medium-high.

Spice Sizzle3 MINS

Heat oil. Add mustard/cumin. When they pop, add garlic and red chilies.

Golden Onions4 MINS

Add onions and curry leaves. Sauté until golden-brown and fragrant.

Final Marriage2 MINS

Open cooker. Add salt & mash dal. Pour hot tadka over and stir well.

Garnish1 MIN

Add coriander. Cover with lid for 1 min to trap the aroma before serving.
