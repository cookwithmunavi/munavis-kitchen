Palak Dal

🕒 30 MINS 👨‍🍳 BEGINNER SAFE 🥗 STEP-BY-STEP

Cooker Items

  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Fresh Spinach 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Medium
  • Green Chilies 2 Nos
  • Tamarind Small piece
  • Water 3 Cups

Tadka Spices

  • Oil / Ghee 2 tbsp
  • Onion 1 Small
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Red Chilies 2 Dry
  • Mustard/Cumin 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves 8 Leaves
  • Coriander Garnish

🛠️ Pre-Check

  • Dal Rinsed? 3 Times
  • Garlic Peeled? Yes
  • Veggies Washed? Twice
  • Cooker Ready? Clean
00:00

TAP A TASK TO START

BEGINNER START Wash & Clean Everything

Rinse Dal 3 times. Wash Spinach in a big bowl to remove dirt. Wash tomatoes, chilies, curry leaves and coriander leaves thoroughly.

The Chopping Board

Peel Garlic & Onion skins. Chop Onion, Tomatoes, and Spinach. Slit green chilies. Crush Garlic cloves with a heavy spoon.

CRITICAL Pressure Cooker Setup

Place Dal, Palak, Tomato, Chilies, Tamarind, Turmeric & Water in cooker. **Do NOT add salt yet.** Cook for 3 whistles.

WATCH OIL Spices Sizzle

Heat oil in a small pan. Add Mustard/Cumin. Wait for them to pop. Add Garlic & Red Chilies. Fry until garlic is nutty.

Onion Sauté

Add onions and curry leaves. Sauté until onions turn golden-brown. Don't let them go black!

Mash & Mix Spices

Open cooker once pressure is gone. **Add Salt now.** Whisk/Mash the dal well. Pour the hot Tadka over it.

GARNISH The Final Freshness

Toss in your freshly chopped coriander leaves. Stir once and cover with a lid for 1 minute before serving.

👨‍🍳 Chef's Secrets for Beginners

  • Water Check: If dal is too thick, add a splash of boiling water (never cold water).
  • Tangy Flavor: No tamarind? Squeeze half a lemon after the flame is OFF.
  • Pro Version: Use Ghee instead of Oil for the Tadka to get a restaurant-style aroma.
