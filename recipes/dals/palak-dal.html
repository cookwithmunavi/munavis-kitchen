Palak Dal

🕒 30 Mins 👨‍🍳 Beginner 🌿 Healthy
Step 1: For the Cooker
  • 0.5 Cup Toor Dal
  • 2 Cups Fresh Palak (Chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (Medium)
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 1 pc Tamarind (Small)
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric & Salt
Step 2: For the Tadka Pan
  • 1 Onion (Small)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic (Crushed)
  • 2 Dry Red Chilies
  • 1 tsp Mustard & Cumin Seeds
  • 8 Curry Leaves
Final Garnish
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander Leaves
Kitchen Tools
  • Pressure Cooker
  • Small Tadka Pan
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Hand Whisk / Masher
00:00

Tap task to start

💡 Beginner Tip: Mashing the cooked dal releases starch, making it naturally creamy!
Rinse Dal

Wash toor dal 3 times until water is clear to remove excess starch.

1:00 MIN
Pressure Cook

Add all "Cooker" ingredients with 2 glasses of water. High flame for 3 whistles, then simmer 5 mins.

15:00 MINS
Release & Mash

Let pressure release naturally. Mash the mixture slightly for a creamy base.

5:00 MINS
💡 Technique: Adding seeds to shimmering oil (tempering) wakes up their essential oils.
Sizzle Aromatics

Heat oil in tadka pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin, garlic, and dry red chilies.

1:00 MIN
Fry Onions

Add curry leaves and onion. Sauté until onions turn golden brown and soft.

3:00 MINS
The Final Simmer

Pour hot tadka over the dal. Simmer for 5 mins. Garnish with fresh coriander.

5:00 MINS

Ready to Eat! 🎉
