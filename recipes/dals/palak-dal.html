🍲 Cook the Dal

18–22 mins
Rinse the dal Rinse ½ cup toor dal 2–3 times until water runs clear. 1 min
Add ingredients & pressure cook
Tomatoes (chopped) 2
Spinach (chopped) 250 gms
Tamarind Lemon-sized
Turmeric ½ tsp
Salt ½ tsp
Water 2 glasses

Transfer everything to a pressure cooker. Close the lid and cook on medium-high flame for 4 whistles, then reduce to low flame and cook for 5 minutes.

Natural release Turn off the flame and let pressure release naturally.
Mash & check Open and lightly mash. Dal should be soft and well cooked.
💡 Add hot water if dal becomes too thick.

🔥 Prepare the Tadka

6–8 mins
Heat oil Heat pan on high flame 20–30 sec
Add garlic Sauté till golden 30–40 sec
Add onions Cook till soft 2–3 min
Add cumin & mustard Let crackle 20–30 sec
Add curry leaves & chilies Fry till aromatic 1–2 min
💡 Keep flame controlled to avoid burning garlic.

🍲 Combine & Simmer

Pour tadka into dal, mix well, and simmer for 5 minutes.

🌿 Garnish & Serve

Garnish with coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
