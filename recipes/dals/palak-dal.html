Palak Dal

Nutrition meets comfort in this beginner-friendly dal recipe.

Ingredients

Servings:
Shopping List
  • 0.5 Cup Toor Dal
  • 2 Cups Fresh Palak (Chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (Medium)
  • 1 Onion (Small)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 1 Small piece Tamarind
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric & Salt
  • 2 Glasses Water
Kitchen Tools
  • Pressure Cooker
  • Tadka Pan
  • Cumin & Mustard Seeds
  • Curry Leaves
00:00

TAP A TASK TO START TIMER

Rinse Dal

Wash ½ cup toor dal 3 times until water is clear.

1:00 MIN
Pressure Cook

Add dal, tomatoes, palak, turmeric, salt, tamarind, and water. High flame for 3 whistles, then simmer 5 mins.

15:00 MINS
Release Pressure

Turn off flame and let cooker cool naturally. Mash the dal lightly once opened.

5:00 MINS
Heat Oil

Place pan, add oil/ghee on medium flame.

0:30 SEC
Fry Garlic

Add crushed garlic and fry until light golden brown.

0:40 SEC
Sauté Onion & Spices

Add onion, cumin, mustard, chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until soft.

3:00 MINS
⚠️ Important: Do not burn the garlic; it should be aromatic and golden.
Combine & Simmer

Pour hot tadka into dal. Add coriander leaves and simmer for 5 mins.

5:00 MINS

Recipe Completed! 🎉

Serve your Palak Dal hot with rice or roti. Enjoy!
