Palak Dal

Ingredients

Step 1: For the Cooker
  • 0.5 Cup Toor Dal
  • 2 Cups Fresh Palak
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric & Salt
Step 2: For the Tadka
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Dry Red Chilies
  • 1 tsp Mustard & Cumin
Kitchen Tools
  • Pressure Cooker
  • Tadka Pan
00:00
💡 Beginner Tip: Mashing the cooked dal releases starch, making the curry creamy without adding cream!
High-Flame Whistling

Pressure cook for 3 whistles. Ensure there is enough water to cover the dal by 2 inches.

💡 Doneness Check: Fry garlic until it smells nutty and looks light golden. If it turns black, it becomes bitter!
Sizzle the Tempering

Heat oil until it shimmers. Add seeds first—they should "pop" immediately.

💡 Serving Suggestion: This dal tastes best when rested for 5 minutes after mixing.
