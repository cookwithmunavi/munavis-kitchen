Palak Dal

🕒 30 MINS 👨‍🍳 BEGINNER 🌿 HEALTHY

🥣 For the Cooker

  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Fresh Palak 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Nos
  • Green Chilies 2 Nos
  • Tamarind 1 Small pc
  • Turmeric & Salt 0.5 tsp

🔥 For the Tadka

  • Onion (Small) 1 No
  • Garlic (Crushed) 4 Cloves
  • Dry Red Chilies 2 Nos
  • Mustard & Cumin 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves 8 Leaves
  • Coriander Leaves Garnish

🛠️ Kitchen Tools

  • Pressure Cooker Main
  • Tadka Pan Small
  • Wooden Spoon Stir
  • Masher / Whisk Creamy
00:00

TAP TASK TO START

Rinse Dal

Wash ½ cup toor dal 3 times until water is clear to remove excess starch.

1:00 MIN
Pressure Cook

Add dal, tomatoes, palak, turmeric, salt, tamarind, and 2 glasses of water. High flame for 3 whistles.

15:00 MINS
Release & Mash

Cool naturally. Mash the dal mixture lightly with a whisk for a creamy texture.

5:00 MINS
Fry Aromatics

Heat oil. Fry crushed garlic and dry red chilies until golden and fragrant.

1:00 MIN
Temper Spices & Onion

Add seeds, curry leaves, and onion. Sauté until onions are translucent.

3:00 MINS
The Final Simmer

Pour hot tadka into dal. Simmer for 5 mins. Top with fresh coriander.

5:00 MINS

Enjoy Your Meal! 🎉
