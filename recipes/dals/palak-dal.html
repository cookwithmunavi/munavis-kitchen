Palak Dal

🕒 30 Mins ⭐ Beginner Safe 🥗 Step-By-Step
Cooker Items
  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Palak 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Medium
  • Green Chilies 2 Nos
  • Tamarind Small piece
  • Water 3 Cups
Tadka Spices
  • Oil / Ghee 2 tbsp
  • Onion 1 Small
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Red Chilies 2 Dry
  • Mustard/Cumin 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves 8 Leaves
Pre-Check
  • Dal Rinsed? 3 Times
  • Palak Clean? Twice
  • Garlic Peeled? Yes
  • Cooker Ready? Ready
Step Completion 0%
0:00

TAP A TASK TO START

Meticulous Prep5 MINS
  • Rinse toor dal 3 times until clear.
  • Wash palak twice to remove hidden grit.
  • Slit green chilies and chop tomatoes.
Pressure Cooking15 MINS
  • Combine dal, palak, tomatoes, and tamarind in cooker.
  • Add 3 cups water. **No salt yet.**
  • Cook for 4 whistles on medium-high heat.
Golden Tempering5 MINS
  • Heat ghee and crackle mustard/cumin seeds.
  • Add garlic; sauté until nutty golden brown.
  • Add onions and curry leaves; sauté until soft.
Mash & Mix3 MINS
  • Release pressure naturally and open cooker.
  • **Add Salt now.** Whisk dal until smooth.
  • Pour hot Tadka directly into the dal.
The Infusion2 MINS
  • Toss in freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  • Cover immediately for 2 minutes to lock aroma.

💡 Chef's Secrets

  • Salt prevents dal from softening—always add it at the very end.
  • Sautéing garlic until golden (not burnt) is key for aroma.
  • Covering the dal immediately locks in the smoky smell.
