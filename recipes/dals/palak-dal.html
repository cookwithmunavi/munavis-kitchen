Palak Dal

A perfect balance of nutrition and comforting flavor.

Rinse Dal
Take ½ cup toor dal. Wash 3 times until water becomes clear.
Add Ingredients
Add tomatoes, spinach, turmeric, salt, tamarind and 2 glasses water.
Pressure Cook
Close cooker lid properly. Keep whistle. Cook on high flame for 3 whistles. Then simmer 5 min.
Release Pressure
Turn off flame and let cooker cool naturally before opening.
Mash Dal
Open lid and mash lightly using spoon.
💡 Tip: Add hot water if dal is too thick.
00:00

Click step

Heat Oil
Place pan, add oil, heat on medium flame.
Add Garlic
Add chopped garlic and fry till light golden.
Add Onion
Cook till soft and slightly brown.
Add Cumin & Mustard
Let them crackle.
Add Curry Leaves
Add green chilies and curry leaves, sauté well.
⚠️ Do not burn garlic.
00:00

Click step

Add Tadka
Pour hot tadka into dal carefully.
Simmer
Cook for 5 minutes on low flame.
⭐ Add ghee for better taste.
Garnish
Add coriander leaves.
Serve
Serve hot with rice or roti.
