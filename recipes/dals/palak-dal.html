Palak Dal

🕒 30 Mins
👨‍🍳 Beginner
🌶️ Mild-Medium

Ingredients

Shopping List
  • 0.5 Cup Toor Dal
  • 2 Cups Palak (Chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 2 Dry Red Chilies
  • 1 tsp Mustard & Cumin
  • 8 Curry Leaves
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric & Salt
Kitchen Tools
  • Pressure Cooker
  • Small Tadka Pan
  • Wooden Spoon
00:00

Tap a task to start

Rinse & Clean

Wash toor dal 3 times until the water is clear.

Pressure Cook

Add dal, palak, tomatoes, chilies, tamarind, turmeric, salt, and water. 3 whistles on high.

Cooldown

Let steam release naturally, then mash slightly.

Fry Garlic & Dry Chilies

Heat oil. Add garlic and red chilies until golden brown.

Temper Spices

Add mustard, cumin, curry leaves, and onion. Sauté until soft.

The Final Simmer

Add tadka to dal and simmer for 5 mins for deep flavor.

Ready to Eat! 🎉
