Palak Dal

🕒 30 MINS ⭐ BEGINNER SAFE 🥗 STEP-BY-STEP
Cooker Items
  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Fresh Spinach 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Nos
  • Water 3 Cups
Tadka Spices
  • Ghee/Oil 2 tbsp
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Red Chilies 2 Dry
  • Coriander Garnish
Pre-Check
  • Dal Rinsed? 3x
  • Spinach Clean? 2x
  • Cooker Ready? Yes
COOKING PROGRESS 0%
0:00

TAP A TASK TO START

Meticulous Prep5 MINS
  • Rinse toor dal 3 times until clear.
  • Wash spinach twice to remove all grit.
  • Slit green chilies and chop tomatoes.
Pressure Cooking15 MINS
  • Combine dal, spinach, and tomatoes in cooker.
  • Add 3 cups water. **CRITICAL: No salt yet.**
  • Cook for 4 whistles on medium heat.
The Tempering5 MINS
  • Heat ghee and crackle mustard/cumin seeds.
  • Add garlic and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add onions and curry leaves; sauté until soft.
Mash & Mix3 MINS
  • Open cooker once pressure is naturally gone.
  • **Add Salt now.** Mash dal well with a whisk.
  • Pour the hot Tadka directly over the mashed dal.
The Final Garnish1 MIN
  • Toss in freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  • Cover with lid for 1 minute to trap aroma.
💡 Chef's Secrets for Beginners
  • Salt prevents dal from softening—always add it at the very end.
  • Don't burn the garlic; golden brown provides the best flavor.
  • Covering the dal after adding tadka locks in the smoky smell.
