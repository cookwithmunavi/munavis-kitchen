Palak Dal

🕒 30 MINS ⭐ BEGINNER FRIENDLY 🌿 FRESH INGREDIENTS

Ready to Cook?

Prep & Ingredients

COOKER BASE
  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Fresh Spinach 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Nos
  • Green Chilies 2 Nos
  • Tamarind 1 Small pc
THE TADKA
  • Oil / Ghee 2 tbsp
  • Onion 1 Small
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Dry Red Chilies 2 Nos
  • Mustard & Cumin 1 tsp
PREP CHECKLIST
  • Wash Dal 3 Times
  • Wash Spinach Twice
  • Peel Garlic/Onion Done
  • Chop Tomatoes Small
00:00

TAP A TASK TO START

Wash & Chop

Wash dal until clear. Rinse spinach in a bowl of water to remove grit. Fine-chop onions and tomatoes.

Pressure Cook

Place dal, spinach, tomatoes, chilies, and 3 cups water in the cooker. 3-4 whistles on medium flame.

Infuse Aromatics

Heat oil. Pop mustard/cumin seeds. Add red chilies and crushed garlic. Fry until garlic smells nutty.

Sauté Onions

Add onions and curry leaves. Sauté on medium heat until the onions are golden and soft.

The Final Mix

Add salt to the cooked dal and mash it gently. Pour the hot tadka over the dal and stir well.

Fresh Garnish

Add freshly chopped coriander. Cover the lid for 1 min to trap the aroma. Serve hot!

ENJOY YOUR MEAL! 🎉
