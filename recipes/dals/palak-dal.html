Palak Dal

🕒 30 MINS 👨‍🍳 BEGINNER 🌿 FRESH VEGGIES

🥘 For the Cooker

  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Fresh Palak 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Nos
  • Green Chilies 2 Nos
  • Tamarind 1 Small pc
  • Turmeric & Salt 0.5 tsp

🍳 For the Tadka

  • Onion (Small) 1 No
  • Garlic (Crushed) 4 Cloves
  • Dry Red Chilies 2 Nos
  • Mustard & Cumin 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves 8 Leaves
  • Coriander Leaves Garnish

🛠️ Kitchen Tools

  • Pressure Cooker Step 1
  • Small Tadka Pan Step 2
  • Wooden Spoon Stirring
  • Masher Creamy Text
00:00

TAP A TASK TO START

Rinse Dal

Wash ½ cup toor dal 3 times until water is clear.

1:00 MIN
Pressure Cook

Combine dal, palak, tomatoes, and water. High flame for 3 whistles.

15:00 MINS
Release & Mash

Let pressure release naturally, then mash for a creamy texture.

5:00 MINS
Fry Aromatics

Heat oil. Fry crushed garlic and dry red chilies until golden.

1:00 MIN
Sauté Onions

Add seeds, curry leaves, and onion. Cook until translucent.

3:00 MINS
The Final Simmer

Combine tadka with dal. Simmer for 5 mins. Top with coriander.

5:00 MINS

Enjoy Your Healthy Meal! 🎉
