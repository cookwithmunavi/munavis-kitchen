Palak Dal

🕒 30 Mins
👨‍🍳 Beginner
🌿 Healthy

Ingredients

Step 1: For the Cooker
  • 0.5 Cup Toor Dal
  • 2 Cups Fresh Palak
  • 2 Tomatoes (Chopped)
  • 2 Green Chilies
  • 1 pc Tamarind
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric & Salt
Step 2: For the Tadka
  • 1 Onion (Finely sliced)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic (Crushed)
  • 2 Dry Red Chilies
  • 1 tsp Mustard & Cumin
  • 8 Curry Leaves
Final Garnish
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Coriander
Kitchen Tools
  • Pressure Cooker
  • Tadka Pan
  • Wooden Spoon
00:00

Tap task to start timer

Cooker Prep

Place all "Cooker" ingredients with 2 glasses of water. Cook for 3 whistles on high flame.

Mash Dal

Once pressure releases, mash the mixture slightly for a creamy base.

The Tempering

Heat oil in tadka pan. Add garlic, dry chilies, seeds, and curry leaves. Sauté onions until golden.

Combine & Garnish

Pour tadka over dal. Simmer for 5 mins. Top with fresh coriander leaves.

Enjoy your Meal! 🍽️
