Palak Dal

Cooker Items
  • Toor Dal 0.5 Cup
  • Fresh Spinach 2 Cups
  • Tomatoes 2 Medium
  • Green Chilies 2 Nos
  • Tamarind Small piece
  • Water 3 Cups
Tadka Spices
  • Oil / Ghee 2 tbsp
  • Onion 1 Small
  • Garlic 4 Cloves
  • Red Chilies 2 Dry
  • Mustard/Cumin 1 tsp
  • Curry Leaves 8 Leaves
  • Coriander Garnish
Pre-Check
  • Dal Rinsed? 3 Times
  • Garlic Peeled? Yes
  • Veggies Washed? Twice
  • Cooker Ready? Clean
0:00

TAP A TASK TO START

Meticulous Prep5 MINS
  • Rinse toor dal 3 times until the water runs clear.
  • Soak spinach in a deep bowl of water twice to remove grit.
  • Slit green chilies lengthwise and roughly chop tomatoes.
Pressure Cooking15 MINS
  • Combine dal, spinach, tomatoes, chilies, and tamarind in cooker.
  • Add 3 cups of water. **CRITICAL: Do not add salt yet.**
  • Cook for 4 whistles on medium-high heat.
Tempering Spices3 MINS
  • Heat ghee in a pan until shimmering.
  • Add mustard/cumin seeds until they crackle fully.
  • Add dry red chilies and crushed garlic; sauté until golden brown.
Sautéing Aromatics4 MINS
  • Add sliced onions and curry leaves to the pan.
  • Sauté on medium-low heat until translucent.
  • Ensure onions remain tender and don't over-brown.
Mash & Mix3 MINS
  • Release pressure naturally and open the cooker lid.
  • **Add Salt now.** Whisk dal until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the hot Tadka directly into the mashed dal.
The Infusion2 MINS
  • Toss in freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  • Stir once and cover with a lid immediately.
  • Rest for 2 minutes to lock in the aromatic steam.
